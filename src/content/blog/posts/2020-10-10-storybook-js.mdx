[Storybook](https://storybook.js.org/) is a UI dev sandbox. You can use it as an isolated environment for prototyping React/Angular/+ components. It's used by Airbnb, Squarespace, Coursera, and [many](https://github.com/search?q=.storybook&type=Code) [others](https://github.com/search?q=.stories.js&type=Code).
